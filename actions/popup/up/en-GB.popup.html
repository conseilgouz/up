Opens a popup after a delay or a position in the page

syntax: {up popup=5s}popup content{/up popup} Open popup 5 seconds after page load
syntax: {up popup=50%}popup content{/up popup} Open popup halfway down the page
syntax: {up popup=#bloc}popup content{/up popup} Opens the popup when the top of the block #block is at the top of the browsers visible area
